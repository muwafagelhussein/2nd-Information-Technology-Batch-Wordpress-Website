About Us

There are 33 members in the 2nd IT Batch, Unfortunately due to the recent closing problems in the university our number decreased significantly, but the students did well in recovering from the damage.
Our Leader is Neam Al-ilah and our coordinator is Shaima Mamoun, we are looking forward to exploring further opportunities and are willing to work hard to improve.

Materials

Principles of Management

As the name suggest, the course talks about the basics and importance of Management and management functions. It also explains the managing challenges and constraints and how to manage in a global environment and how to manage diversities, change and innovation.

New Information Technology

Includes all new web technologies and computer languages and how to apply them.

principles of Digital circuits

Shows the circuits inside the computer and how to logically use them.

Principals of Economy

Talks about the economy of the country, its expenses and its earnings.

Files management

Explains the relationship between memory and storage devices and how data transfers between them.

Fundamentals of Programming

Java programming Language and it’s syntax and Object Oriented Programming.

Time Table

We often had classes everyday except Friday and Sunday.

Events

The school has made many events and occasions to celeprate and some of the batch participated in them like Pi day, chess challenges or basketball games. other events like Writing day or Poems day included other batches . the school also organized workshops to learn soft skills like UI/UX design and many others.

Sports

Many sports were available for anyone who wanted to play or watch. Including Football, basketball and volley ball games.

We Are Family

The university is more than studying, it’s also about making bonds and friendships, we all learn together as friends, and we support each other during struggles, making sure each one of us carries the other to succeed.

“Family isn’t always your relatives. It’s the ones who accept you for who you are. The ones who would do anything to see you smile, and who love you no matter what.”
